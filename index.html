About Us

Building more
than structures

Taephers Resources Private Limited is a leading provider of construction, engineering and green-energy solutions across Zimbabwe.

We work across engineering and architectural design, construction, infrastructure renovation and maintenance, solar installation, and waste management. Based in Harare, our team is dedicated to using advanced technology and sustainable methods on every residential, commercial and industrial project we take on.

Our subsidiary, Noziula Design Group, supports our operations with specialised engineering and architectural services — giving every project the precision it needs from drawing board to handover.

We believe in building more than just structures; we're building a greener, more sustainable future for Zimbabwe.

Noziula Design Group Our architectural & engineering subsidiary — precision drawings, structural analysis, and 3D visualisation for every build.

What We Do

Six ways we
put a plan into ground

From the first survey peg to the last coat of paint — and the solar panel on the roof.

01

Construction

Residential, commercial and industrial building — with full project management, site supervision and cost control from groundbreaking to handover.

02

Engineering & Design

Structural engineering and architectural design through Noziula Design Group — building design, 3D visualisation, foundations and tendering documentation.

03

GIS & Town Planning

Spatial data, mapping and land-use planning that inform urban design, zoning and infrastructure planning for balanced, sustainable growth.

04

Green Energy

Solar panel installation and maintenance, energy audits and efficiency consulting, plus wind-turbine upkeep for renewable systems that last.

05

Renovation & Maintenance

Home renovation, painting, and upkeep of solar panels and wind turbines — keeping structures and systems performing long after handover.

06

Waste Management

Turning organic and non-organic waste into biogas and electricity through anaerobic digestion — reducing landfill and cutting emissions.
